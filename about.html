About Us

  • Aim
  • Our Journey
  • Vision
  • To foster amongst students the will to connect with their peers, their elders, the community and the environment.
  • To provide a platform to learn and grow
  • To encourage the youth to be active citizens that wish to make a change by applying their skills to social problems
  • To provide support, information, and hope to underprivileged communities
  • To assist and collaborate with various organizations working towards our common goal.
  • To contribute by fundraising for the underprivileged section of society
  • To use education as an effective tool to achieve our vision
Seva ICT- Started with Dr. Ruhi Foundation School 2015, moved to online platform 2020 Seva Prayaas- Started with Shahpur Prathmik Vidyalaya 2016, moved to online 2020, got Dr. Ruhi Foundation School also into fold 2021
We dream of a day each individual will embrace the upliftment of the community as a personal responsibility. This is a humble attempt to offer support to underprivileged communities whole learning, growing and finding a purpose as a student.
