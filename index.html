Who are we?

Seva is a student-led community outreach program. We aim to inculcate in all students the values of sensitivity, respect and gratitude by using education as an effective tool to achieve our vision, promoting collaboration amongst students and with peers, elders, other organisations and the community in its entirety.Â Our mission is to create a self reliant society of active citizens who are caring and compassionate individuals.
We work with underprivileged children and government primary schools like the Dr. Ruhi Foundation School. Our team is divided into two programs: ICT and Prayaas.
Prayaas is involved in teaching academics in an interactive manner, teaching performaning and visual arts and sports.
ICT takes classes to teach chldren basic computer skills.
We also conduct montly awareness campaigns with our middle school volunteers to spread awareness on topics like food wastage that have an impact on our societal well being.
